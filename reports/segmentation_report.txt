-- Important Caveat --
  This dataset's ~99% repeat-customer rate (noted in Module 7) is a known artifact of the synthetic data generation method (customers were resampled per order rather than modeling realistic customer acquisition/churn). In a real dataset with a genuine mix of one-time and repeat buyers, segment sizes and separation would look meaningfully different -- treat these specific proportions as illustrative of the METHOD, not as a real business finding.
